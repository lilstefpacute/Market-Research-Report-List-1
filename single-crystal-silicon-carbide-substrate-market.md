<p><p>Single crystal silicon carbide substrate market types include conductive substrate and semi-insulating substrate. Conductive substrate refers to a silicon carbide substrate that has a high electrical conductivity, allowing for efficient electrical conduction. It is commonly used in electronic devices and power electronics. On the other hand, semi-insulating substrate refers to a silicon carbide substrate that has a high electrical resistivity, preventing or minimizing electrical conduction. It is mainly used in applications that require electrical insulation, such as high-frequency devices and microwave equipment.</p></p>

<p><p>The micro glass aspheric lens market encompasses various types of lenses, each with its unique shape and characteristics. Flat lenses have a flat surface on both sides, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal refraction. Biconvex lenses have a curved surface on both sides, bending light towards a central focal point. Double concave lenses have two inwardly curved surfaces, diverging light rays. Concave lenses possess one curved inward surface, often used to correct nearsightedness. Lastly, convex lenses have one outwardly curved surface, converging light rays towards a focal point.</p></p>

<p><p>Desiccant rotors are commonly used in air conditioning systems to remove moisture from the air. There are three main types of desiccant materials used in these rotors - silica gel, molecular sieve, and others. Silica gel is the most frequently used desiccant material, known for its high adsorption capacity and stability. Molecular sieve desiccant rotors are used for applications that require higher levels of humidity control, as they have a narrower pore size distribution. The "other" category includes desiccant materials such as activated alumina or combinations of different desiccants.</p></p>

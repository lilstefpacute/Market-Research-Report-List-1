<p><p>The E-scooter sharing market can be divided into two types: dockless and station-based. In a dockless market, users can locate and rent electric scooters through mobile apps without the need for specific docking stations. This provides flexibility in parking and allows users to drop off the scooter wherever convenient within designated areas. On the other hand, in a station-based market, users must pick up and return the scooters at specific docking stations. This ensures organized parking and availability of the scooters at designated locations.</p></p>

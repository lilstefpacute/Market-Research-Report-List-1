<p><p>Semiconductor gas filters can be categorized into two main types: filter type and disposable type. </p><p>Filter type semiconductor gas filters are designed to remove impurities and particles from the gas stream by trapping them within the filter material. These filters are typically reusable and require periodic cleaning to maintain their effectiveness.</p><p>On the other hand, disposable type semiconductor gas filters are designed for one-time use. These filters are usually made of a disposable material that does not allow for cleaning or reuse. Once the filter becomes clogged or saturated, it needs to be replaced entirely.</p><p>Both filter types serve the purpose of purifying the gas stream in semiconductor manufacturing processes, but they differ in terms of longevity and maintenance requirements.</p></p>

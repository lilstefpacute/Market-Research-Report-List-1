<p><p>Rice combine harvesters are agricultural machines used to harvest rice crops. There are three main types of rice combine harvesters in the market. Drum threshing rice combine harvesters use a rotating drum to separate grain from the straw. Axial-flow rice combine harvesters use a combination of axial and tangential flow to separate grain efficiently. Hybrid rice combine harvesters combine the features of both drum threshing and axial-flow harvesters for better performance. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of rice farmers in optimizing their harvesting process.</p></p>

<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Commercial Vehicle Steering Systems market?</strong></p>
<p><p>Some emerging trends in the global commercial vehicle steering systems market include the adoption of electric power steering (EPS) technology, the integration of advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) with steering systems, and the use of steer-by-wire technology. EPS offers improved fuel efficiency and reduced maintenance costs, while ADAS integration enhances safety and control. Steer-by-wire technology eliminates the physical connection between the steering wheel and the wheels, enabling more flexibility in vehicle design. Additionally, there is a growing focus on the development of autonomous vehicles, which will require advanced steering systems to ensure effective control and maneuverability.</p></p>

<p><strong>The PE Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>PE foam, or Polyethylene foam, is a versatile material used in various industries. There are three main types of PE foam in the market - IXPE, XPE, and EPE. IXPE (Irradiation Cross-linked Polyethylene) foam is known for its high thermal insulation properties and resistance to chemicals. XPE (Cross-linked Polyethylene) foam is lightweight and offers good shock absorption, making it suitable for packaging and cushioning applications. EPE (Expanded Polyethylene) foam is durable and flexible, often used for protecting fragile items during shipping and handling. These different types of PE foam cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>

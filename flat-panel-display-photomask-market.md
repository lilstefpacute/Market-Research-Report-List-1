<p><p>The flat panel display photomask market comprises different types of masks used in the manufacturing of flat panel displays. Quartz masks are made from quartz glass, offering high precision and durability. Soda masks are made from soda lime glass, providing cost-effective solutions for larger display sizes. Relief plates have raised areas to create a pattern on the display surface. Other market players offer various types of masks, such as chromium and glass-based masks, each with their unique characteristics and applications. These masks play a crucial role in accurately transferring patterns during the display production process.</p></p>

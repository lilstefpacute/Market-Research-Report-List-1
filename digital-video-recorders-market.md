<p><p>Digital video recorders (DVRs) are devices used to capture and store video footage for later viewing or analysis. There are three main types of DVRs in the market. Firstly, embedded DVRs are standalone devices with a built-in operating system and storage, offering a simple setup. Secondly, hybrid DVRs combine the features of both embedded DVRs and network video recorders, enabling integration with IP cameras and offering scalable solutions. Lastly, PC-based DVRs require a computer to function and provide flexibility in terms of storage capacity and software options.</p></p>

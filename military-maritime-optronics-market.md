<p><p>The military maritime optronics market comprises different types, including multispectral and hyperspectral. Multispectral technology enhances visual perception by capturing and processing images across various wavelengths, allowing for improved target detection and identification. On the other hand, hyperspectral technology captures and processes images across hundreds of narrow spectral bands, providing detailed information about the composition and characteristics of objects. Both technologies enable military forces to gain a tactical advantage by enhancing situational awareness, reconnaissance, surveillance, and target acquisition capabilities in maritime environments.</p></p>

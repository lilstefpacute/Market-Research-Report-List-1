<p><strong>The Injection Epoxy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity</li><li>Super Low Viscosity</li><li>Medium Viscosity</li><li>High Viscosity</li><li>Extra High Viscosity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Injection Epoxy market consists of various types based on their viscosity levels. Low viscosity epoxy has a thinner consistency and is suitable for smaller cracks. Super low viscosity epoxy is even thinner, making it ideal for narrow and tight cracks. Medium viscosity epoxy strikes a balance between flow and stability, suitable for both narrow and wider cracks. High viscosity epoxy is thicker, ideal for larger cracks. Lastly, extra high viscosity epoxy is the thickest and most suitable for extremely wide and deep cracks.</p></p>

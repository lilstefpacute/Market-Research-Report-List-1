<p><p>The industrial and marine steam turbine market consists of two main types: condensing steam turbines and non-condensing steam turbines. A condensing steam turbine operates by converting steam into liquid as it passes through the turbine, achieving higher efficiency levels. On the other hand, a non-condensing steam turbine allows the steam to exit the turbine without condensing it, resulting in lower overall efficiency. These two types of steam turbines are used in various industries and marine applications to generate power and propulsion systems.</p></p>

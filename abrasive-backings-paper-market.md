<p><p>Abrasive backing papers are classified into two main types: dry abrasive paper and wet abrasive paper. Dry abrasive paper is designed for use with dry sanding applications, where the abrasive particles are bonded to the paper using a resin. It is suitable for tasks such as woodworking, metalworking, and general surface preparation. Wet abrasive paper, on the other hand, is intended for wet sanding applications and is typically used in automotive refinishing, painting, and polishing tasks. It has a waterproof backing that allows it to be used in conjunction with water or other liquids for improved performance.</p></p>

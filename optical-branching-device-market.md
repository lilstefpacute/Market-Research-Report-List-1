FBT couplers are made by fusing and tapering two or more fibers together, enabling signal splitting with high reliability. PLC splitters, on the other hand, use a flat waveguide structure to split signals, offering more precise splitting ratios and better performance. Both FBC and PLC technologies play significant roles in the optical branching device market, catering to different needs of network applications.</p></p>

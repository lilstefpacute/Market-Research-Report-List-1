<p><p>The pedal motorcycle market is divided into two main types: two-stroke motorcycles and four-stroke motorcycles. Two-stroke motorcycles are powered by an engine that completes the combustion process in two strokes of the piston. These motorcycles are known for their high power-to-weight ratio and are commonly used in off-road and racing applications. On the other hand, four-stroke motorcycles have an engine that completes the combustion process in four strokes, offering better fuel efficiency and smoother power delivery. These motorcycles are more commonly used for everyday commuting and transportation purposes.</p></p>

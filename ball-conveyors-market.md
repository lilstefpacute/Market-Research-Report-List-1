<p><p>Ball conveyors are a type of material handling equipment used to transport items from one location to another in a smooth and efficient manner. There are two main types of ball conveyors: stationary type and extendible type. Stationary ball conveyors remain fixed in one position, typically used for small-scale applications. On the other hand, extendible ball conveyors can be retracted or extended as needed, allowing for flexibility in transporting goods across longer distances. Both types of ball conveyors enable improved productivity and ease of handling in various industries.</p></p>

The growing adoption of ADAS and the increasing focus on vehicle safety are expected to drive the demand for soft piezoelectric ceramics in this sector.</p><p>In the healthcare sector, soft piezoelectric ceramics are used in medical imaging devices, ultrasonic transducers, and wearable healthcare devices. <p><p>The Soft Piezoelectric Ceramics market is segmented into two types: PZT5A and PZT5H. PZT5A is a type of soft piezoelectric ceramic material that has high sensitivity, low coercive field, and good temperature stability. It is widely used in sensors, actuators, and other devices that require high performance. On the other hand, PZT5H is another soft piezoelectric ceramic that offers high electromechanical coupling coefficient and excellent piezoelectric properties. It is commonly used in ultrasonic transducers, ultrasound imaging, and other medical applications. Both PZT5A and PZT5H have significant importance in the soft piezoelectric ceramics market due to their unique features and applications.</p></p>

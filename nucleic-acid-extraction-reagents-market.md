<p><p>Nucleic acid extraction reagents are used to isolate DNA or RNA from biological samples. These reagents are essential for various research and diagnostic applications. CTAB/NaCl solution is a detergent-based reagent for extracting DNA from plant tissues. Chloroform/Isoamyl Alcohol and Phenol/Chloroform/Isoamyl Alcohol are organic solvents used for DNA purification. Isopropanol and ethanol are alcohol-based precipitants used to separate nucleic acids from other molecules.</p></p>

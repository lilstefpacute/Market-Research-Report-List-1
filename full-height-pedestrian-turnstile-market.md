<p><p>The Full Height Pedestrian Turnstile market encompasses two main types: the One-way Channel and the Two-way Channel. The One-way Channel turnstile allows passage in one direction only and is commonly used in areas where the flow of pedestrians needs to be regulated strictly, such as subway stations or airports. On the other hand, the Two-way Channel turnstile permits passage in both directions and is suitable for areas with bidirectional pedestrian traffic, like shopping malls or stadiums. Both types fulfill the purpose of ensuring controlled access and enhancing security in various environments.</p></p>

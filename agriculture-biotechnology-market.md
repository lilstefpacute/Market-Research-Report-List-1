<p><p>Agriculture biotechnology refers to the application of scientific techniques and tools to modify plants, animals, and microorganisms for improving their efficiency and sustainability in agricultural practices. One trend is the increasing focus on precision agriculture, which involves the use of technologies like remote sensing, GPS, and data analytics to optimize farming practices and improve resource efficiency. These advancements aim to improve animal health, productivity, and disease resistance, ultimately benefiting the livestock industry.</p><p>Overall, the agriculture biotechnology market holds immense potential for addressing global food security and sustainability challenges.

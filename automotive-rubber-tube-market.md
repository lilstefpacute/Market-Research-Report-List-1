<p><p>The automotive rubber tube market is categorized into two types: non-reinforced rubber tube and metal-reinforced rubber tube. A non-reinforced rubber tube is made solely of rubber without any additional reinforcement materials. It is used in applications that require flexibility and resistance to compression. On the other hand, a metal-reinforced rubber tube has a layer of metal embedded within the rubber material for added strength and stability. This type of tube is commonly used in applications that require higher pressure resistance or protection against external forces.</p></p>

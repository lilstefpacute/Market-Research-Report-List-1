<p><ul><li>Digital Power Analyzer</li><li>Power Quality Analyser</li><li>Clamp-on Power Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power analyzer market comprises three main types of products: digital power analyzers, power quality analyzers, and clamp-on power analyzers. Digital power analyzers are used to measure and analyze various electrical parameters such as voltage, current, power, and energy consumption. Power quality analyzers are designed to assess the quality and stability of electrical power by analyzing factors like harmonics, voltage fluctuations, and transients. Clamp-on power analyzers are portable devices that can measure electrical parameters without the need for physical contact with the power lines, making them suitable for on-site analysis and troubleshooting.</p></p>

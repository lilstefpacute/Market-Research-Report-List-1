<p><p>The absorbable hemostats market includes different types of products that are used to stop bleeding during surgical procedures. These types include cellulose, gelatin, fibrin, and others. Cellulose-based hemostats are made from plant sources and are known for their high absorbency and biocompatibility. Gelatin-based hemostats are derived from animal sources and form a gel-like substance when in contact with blood. Fibrin-based hemostats are made from human or animal blood components and promote clotting. Other types of absorbable hemostats may include synthetic materials or combinations of these materials.</p></p>

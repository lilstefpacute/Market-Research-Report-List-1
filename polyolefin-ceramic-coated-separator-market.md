Both types offer enhanced thermal stability, reduced shrinkage, and improved resistance to puncturing, thus reducing the risk of battery failure. These separators are widely used in the battery industry due to their ability to withstand high temperatures, minimize electrolyte leakage, and prevent short circuits, ensuring reliable and efficient battery operation.</p></p>

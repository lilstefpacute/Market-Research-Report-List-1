<p><p>Textile coatings refer to the process of applying a protective layer on textiles to enhance their properties. The market is categorized into three main types: thermoplastics, thermosets, and others. Thermoplastics are polymers that can be melted and reformed multiple times, offering flexibility and recyclability. Thermosets, on the other hand, undergo a chemical reaction during the curing process, resulting in a rigid and durable coating. The "others" category includes coatings made from natural materials or special formulations. These different types cater to various industrial and consumer applications, providing unique benefits in terms of flexibility, durability, and environmental impact.</p></p>

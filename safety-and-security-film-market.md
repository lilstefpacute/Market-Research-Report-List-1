<p><p>Safety and security film market is divided into two types: interior and exterior. Interior safety and security films are installed on the inside of windows and glass surfaces and are designed to hold shattered glass in place, preventing injuries and reducing the risk of burglary or break-ins. They are commonly used in commercial buildings, schools, and homes. On the other hand, exterior safety and security films are applied on the outside of windows and provide an added layer of protection against forced entry, extreme weather conditions, and UV radiation. They are widely used in areas prone to hurricanes, tornadoes, or vandalism.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Air Horn</li><li>Electric Horn</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile horn systems market comprises two main types: air horns and electric horns. Air horns operate by using compressed air to create a loud sound. These horns are usually used in large vehicles like trucks and trains. Electric horns, on the other hand, produce sound by electrically activating a diaphragm or electromagnetic coil. They are generally used in smaller vehicles like cars and motorcycles. Both types of horns serve the purpose of providing a warning signal to other drivers or pedestrians on the road.</p></p>

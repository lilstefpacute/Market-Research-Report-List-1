<p><p>Anisotropic Magnetoresistive (AMR) Sensors are types of magnetic field sensors that are used to measure the changes in the magnetic field. These sensors are used in various applications such as automotive, industrial automation, consumer electronics, aerospace, and defense, among others.</p><p>AMR sensors are known for their high sensitivity, low hysteresis, and small size, which make them ideal for various applications. These sensors work on the principle of the anisotropic magnetoresistance effect, where the electrical resistance of a material changes when it is subjected to a magnetic field. This change in resistance is measured by the sensor and converted into an electrical signal.</p><p>The global AMR sensors market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving the market growth include increasing demand for sensors in the automotive industry for applications such as anti-lock braking systems (ABS) and tire pressure monitoring systems (TPMS), rising adoption of automation in industries, and growing use of consumer electronics products.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology such as the development of miniaturized and cost-effective sensors are further fueling the market growth.

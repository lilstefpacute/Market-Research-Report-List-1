<p><p>The CF therapeutics market includes various types of treatments for cystic fibrosis (CF). Pancreatic enzyme supplements aid digestion by replacing the deficient enzymes in CF patients. Mucolytics help thin and clear the mucus accumulated in the lungs. Bronchodilators are used to relax and open the airways, improving breathing. CFTR modulators are a newer type of treatment that targets the defective protein causing CF, helping to improve the function of the CFTR protein. These treatments collectively aim to manage symptoms, prevent complications, and improve the quality of life for CF patients.</p></p>

<p><p>The mobile surgery table market encompasses different types of tables designed for performing surgeries. Electric tables are powered by electricity, allowing for easy adjustment of height and position. Manual tables require manual operation for adjusting height and position. Hydraulic tables use hydraulic systems to enable smooth movement and positioning. Electro-hydraulic tables combine the features of both electric and hydraulic tables, providing precise control through electrical means and hydraulic power. These various types cater to different surgical requirements and preferences, allowing surgeons to efficiently and comfortably perform procedures.</p></p>

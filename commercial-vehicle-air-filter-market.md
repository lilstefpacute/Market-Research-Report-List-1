<p><ul><li>Dry-type Air Filter</li><li>Wet-type Air Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle air filter market is divided into two types: dry-type air filters and wet-type air filters. Dry-type air filters work by trapping dust and other contaminants in a dry, fibrous media, which can be easily cleaned or replaced. Wet-type air filters, on the other hand, use a wet media that can capture smaller particles and provide better filtration efficiency. Both types of filters help to improve the air quality entering the vehicle's engine, ensuring optimal performance and prolonging the lifespan of the engine.</p></p>

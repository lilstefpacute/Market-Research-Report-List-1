<p><p>EM Brake Market Types:</p><p>1. Single Face Brake: It is a type of electromagnetic brake with a single friction face, commonly used in applications where low torque is required.</p><p>2. Power Off Brake: Power off brakes are electromagnetic brakes that use spring pressure to engage when power is removed. These brakes are often used for emergency stopping or holding applications.</p><p>3. Particle Brake: Particle brakes use magnetic particles to create resistance, allowing for precise control over torque levels. They are commonly used in applications that require adjustable torque without the need for external power.</p><p>4. Hysteresis Power Brake: Hysteresis brakes use magnetic hysteresis to generate torque. They offer smooth and precise torque control, making them ideal for applications that require accurate speed regulation.</p><p>5. Multiple Disk Brake: Multiple disk brakes consist of multiple friction disks stacked together. They provide high torque capacity and are commonly used in heavy-duty industrial applications where braking performance is crucial.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Boom Truck with Underlift</li><li>Wheel-lift Tow Truck</li><li>Integrated Tow Truck</li><li>Flatbed with Wheel-Lift</li><li>Lift Flatbed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The breakdown truck market consists of various types of trucks used for towing and recovering vehicles. Boom trucks with underlift feature a hydraulic boom arm and an underlift that can lift vehicles from the front or rear. Wheel-lift tow trucks use a hydraulic arm and a wheel-lift attachment to lift the towed vehicle. Integrated tow trucks have a built-in tow platform and lifting capabilities. Flatbed with wheel-lift combines a flatbed for carrying vehicles and a wheel-lift for towing. Lift flatbeds have a lifting mechanism to load vehicles onto the flatbed from the ground.</p></p>

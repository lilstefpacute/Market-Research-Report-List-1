<p><p>The Agricultural Robotic Manure Scraper market can be categorized into two types: Straight market and U-shaped market. </p><p>In a Straight market, the manure scraper moves in a straight line along the length of the barn, removing manure from the floor and depositing it into a designated area. This type of market is ideal for barns with a single aisle or narrow spaces.</p><p>On the other hand, a U-shaped market is designed for barns with a central feed alley. The manure scraper moves in a U-shaped pattern, cleaning the floor along the alley and depositing the manure in a specific location. This market type is suited for barns with wider spaces and multiple aisles.</p></p>

<p><p>Valve gear boxes are mechanical devices used to control the movement and operation of valves in various industrial processes. There are two main types of valve gear boxes in the market. Worm gear boxes utilize a worm screw and a worm wheel to transmit motion and power, providing high reduction ratios and smooth operation. On the other hand, bevel gear boxes use bevel gears that intersect at an angle, allowing for efficient transmission of power between shafts in non-parallel positions. Both types of gear boxes have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications.</p></p>

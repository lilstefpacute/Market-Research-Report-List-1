<p><p>Running track systems are categorized into different types based on their composition and construction. The Full PUR System involves applying a polyurethane material over the track surface, providing excellent shock absorption and durability. The Sandwich System consists of a pre-fabricated rubber layer that is glued to a base layer, offering good cushioning and traction. The Spray-Coat System involves spraying a mix of rubber and polyurethane onto the track surface, creating a seamless and resilient track. Other types include latex-based systems, rubber sheet systems, and prefabricated track systems, each offering unique benefits for different athletic needs.</p></p>

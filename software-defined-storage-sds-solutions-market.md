<p><p>Software Defined Storage (SDS) Solutions refers to a data storage architecture that separates the management and provisioning of storage hardware from the software that manages it. The increasing adoption of cloud computing and virtualization technologies is driving the demand for SDS solutions. Moreover, the exponential growth of data and the need for efficient storage management solutions are also contributing to the market's growth.</p><p>One key trend in the SDS market is the integration of SDS with hyper-converged infrastructure (HCI). HCI combines compute, storage, networking, and virtualization into a single hardware appliance, and when combined with SDS, it offers a more simplified and efficient approach to storage management.</p><p>Additionally, the growing demand for data analytics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning technologies is also expected to drive the adoption of SDS solutions. These technologies require storage systems that are scalable, flexible, and capable of handling large volumes of data, which SDS solutions can provide.</p><p>However, the market also faces some challenges.

<p><p>Pesticide inert ingredients are substances added to pesticide formulations to enhance their effectiveness or help deliver the active ingredients. The market for pesticide inert ingredients can be divided into two types: dry and liquid. Dry pesticide inert ingredients are in powder or granular form and are typically mixed with the active ingredients before application. Liquid pesticide inert ingredients, on the other hand, are in liquid form and are usually added to the pesticide formulation as a solution or emulsion. Both types of inert ingredients play a vital role in improving the performance and safety of pesticides.</p></p>

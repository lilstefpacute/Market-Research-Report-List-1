Single-Effect Rising Film Evaporators refer to a system that consists of a single evaporator unit, resulting in a lower evaporation efficiency. On the other hand, Multi-Effect Rising Film Evaporators utilize multiple evaporator units in a series, allowing for the reuse of vapor and offering higher energy efficiency. These different types cater to various industry needs and requirements for efficient evaporation processes.</p></p>

<p><p>The benchtop flammability tester market comprises two main types: vertical type and horizontal type. The vertical type is designed to test the vertical flame spread of fabrics and other materials when exposed to an ignition source. On the other hand, the horizontal type evaluates the horizontal flame spread along the surface of a material. Both types play a crucial role in assessing the fire safety properties of various products, providing valuable information to industries such as textiles, automotive, and furniture manufacturing.</p></p>

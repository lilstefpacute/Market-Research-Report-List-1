<p><strong>The Acrylic Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic sheets market is divided into two main types: extruded acrylic sheets and cast acrylic sheets. Extruded acrylic sheets are manufactured by a continuous extrusion process, resulting in a rigid and lightweight material. They are commonly used for signs, displays, and lighting applications. On the other hand, cast acrylic sheets are produced by pouring liquid acrylic into a mold, resulting in a more durable and optically clear material. They are often used for aquariums, architectural glazing, and medical devices. Both types have their own unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Micro-encapsulation is a technique that involves enclosing small particles or droplets within a protective shell material. The shell material is typically made of polymers, resins, or lipids and serves to protect the core material from degradation, improve stability, and control release properties.</p><p>The micro-encapsulation shell material market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand from various industries such as pharmaceutical, food and beverages, personal care, and agrochemicals. In the pharmaceutical industry, micro-encapsulation is used to improve drug stability, control release, and enhance bioavailability. In the food and beverages industry, it is utilized for flavor masking, controlled release of ingredients, and enhancement of product shelf-life. Moreover, in the personal care industry, micro-encapsulation is employed for fragrance and active ingredient delivery.</p><p>The market analysis suggests that the micro-encapsulation shell material market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period. Researchers are exploring natural polymers and other environmentally friendly materials to develop sustainable micro-encapsulation solutions.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the expansion of applications beyond traditional industries.

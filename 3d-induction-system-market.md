<p><strong>The 3D Induction System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Binocular Stereo Vision</li><li>3D Structure Light</li><li>TOF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D induction system market can be categorized into three types: binocular stereo vision, 3D structure light, and Time of Flight (TOF). Binocular stereo vision uses two cameras to capture images and calculate depth perception, enabling precise 3D measurements. The 3D structure light method projects a pattern onto the object and analyzes deformations to generate a 3D image. TOF technology measures the time taken for light to bounce back from the object, providing accurate distance measurements. These market types offer different approaches to 3D induction systems, catering to various application needs.</p></p>

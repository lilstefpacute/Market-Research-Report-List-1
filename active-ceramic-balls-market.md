<p><p>The Active Ceramic Balls Market includes two main types: Filled Ceramic Balls and Milled Ceramic Balls. Filled Ceramic Balls are composed of a ceramic core filled with various materials, such as activated carbon or zeolite. They are used in applications like water filtration and air purification. On the other hand, Milled Ceramic Balls are finely powdered ceramic materials that have been milled to achieve specific sizes and shapes. These balls are commonly used in industries like electronics and aerospace for their excellent heat resistance and electrical properties.</p></p>

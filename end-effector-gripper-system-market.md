<p><p>The end-effector gripper system market consists of four main types: Electric Grippers, Pneumatic Grippers, Vacuum Grippers/Suction Cups, and Magnetic Grippers. Electric Grippers use electrical power to grip and release objects, providing precise control and adaptable gripping force. Pneumatic Grippers rely on compressed air to operate, offering fast and strong gripping capabilities. Vacuum Grippers/Suction Cups create a vacuum seal to hold objects, suitable for gripping flat or non-porous surfaces. Magnetic Grippers use magnetic fields to attract and hold objects, often used for handling ferromagnetic materials. These types cater to various applications and industries, offering versatility and efficiency.</p></p>

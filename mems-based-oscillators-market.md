<p><p>The MEMS-based oscillators market is segmented into various types. Voltage Control Oscillator (VCXO) adjusts its output frequency based on an applied voltage. Temperature Compensated Oscillator (TCXO) uses a compensating circuit to counter temperature variations. Microcontroller Compensated Crystal Oscillators (MCXO) employs a microcontroller to enhance stability and accuracy. Spread Spectrum Oscillator (SSXO) spreads the frequency spectrum to reduce electromagnetic interference. Frequency Select Oscillator (FSXO) offers multiple frequency outputs. Digitally Controlled Oscillator (DCXO) allows precise frequency control through digital inputs. Additionally, there are other types of MEMS-based oscillators available in the market serving various applications.</p></p>

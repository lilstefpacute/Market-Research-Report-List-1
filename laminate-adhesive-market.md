<p><p>Laminate Adhesive Market Types refer to different adhesive formulations used in the lamination process. Solvent-based laminate adhesive contains solvents that evaporate during curing, providing good bonding strength. Solventless laminate adhesive, on the other hand, does not contain solvents and offers high bond strength without the need for solvent evaporation. Water-based laminate adhesive uses water as a carrier, making it environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. These adhesive types cater to different industries and applications, providing options for manufacturers based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>

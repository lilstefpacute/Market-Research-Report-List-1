<p><p>Electric watercraft refers to boats or water vehicles that are powered by electricity rather than traditional fossil fuel engines. There are three main types of electric watercraft on the market: battery electric watercraft, plug-in hybrid electric watercraft, and hybrid electric watercraft. Battery electric watercraft solely rely on rechargeable batteries for power. Plug-in hybrid electric watercraft can be charged through an electrical outlet, but they also have a backup combustion engine. Hybrid electric watercraft combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, allowing for more flexibility in power sources.</p></p>

The touchless functionality of these seats reduces the risk of contamination and improves overall bathroom hygiene.</p><p>Overall, the electronic toilet seat market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years, driven by consumer demand for advanced bathroom fixtures, growing awareness about hygiene, and technological advancements in the industry.</p></p>

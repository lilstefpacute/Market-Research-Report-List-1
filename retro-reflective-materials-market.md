<p><p>Retro-reflective materials are widely used in various industries for enhanced visibility and safety purposes. The market can primarily be categorized into two types: microprismatic and glass beads. Microprismatic retro-reflective materials consist of small prisms that reflect light back to its source, making them highly effective in low-light conditions. Glass beads, on the other hand, are spherical particles that reflect light in many directions, making them ideal for applications requiring wide-angle reflective properties. Both types of retro-reflective materials play a crucial role in improving visibility and ensuring safety in various industries and applications.</p></p>

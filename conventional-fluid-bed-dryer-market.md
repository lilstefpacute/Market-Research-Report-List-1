<p><p>The Conventional Fluid Bed Dryer market includes two types: Batch Operation and Continuous Operation. In the Batch Operation market, the dryer consists of a drying chamber where a set quantity of material is loaded and processed in batches. This is suitable for small-scale operations with diverse products. On the other hand, in the Continuous Operation market, the dryer operates continuously, with a constant feed of material and a simultaneous discharge of the dried product. This type of dryer is more suitable for large-scale operations with a consistent product stream.</p></p>

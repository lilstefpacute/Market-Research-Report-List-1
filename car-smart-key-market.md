<p><p>The car smart key market includes two main types: Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES). PKES allows drivers to access their vehicles without using a physical key by simply approaching the car. It utilizes radio frequency identification (RFID) technology. On the other hand, RKES relies on a remote control to lock, unlock, and sometimes start the vehicle from a certain distance. Both systems enhance convenience and security by incorporating advanced technology into the automotive industry.</p></p>

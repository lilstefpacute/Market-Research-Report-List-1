<p><p>Commercial Use Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery (MVHR) Units are used to provide fresh air and extract stale air in commercial buildings. There are three main types of MVHR units: ceiling-mount, wall-mount, and cabinet-mount. </p><p>Ceiling-mount units are installed in the ceiling and are often preferred in buildings with limited space. </p><p>Wall-mount units are mounted on the wall and are suitable for both new construction and retrofit applications. </p><p>Cabinet-mount units are installed in a cabinet or ducted enclosure, allowing for easy accessibility and maintenance.</p><p>All these types of MVHR units aim to improve indoor air quality while recapturing and reusing heat from the extracted air to save energy and reduce heating costs.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Wire-Cut Flushing Type</li><li>Wire-Cut Submerge Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wire-Cut Electrical Discharge Machine (EDM) market consists of two main types: Wire-Cut Flushing Type and Wire-Cut Submerge Type. In the Wire-Cut Flushing Type, the wire electrode used for cutting is exposed to the air, and a continuous flow of flushing liquid is used to remove debris and cool the workpiece in the cutting zone. On the other hand, in the Wire-Cut Submerge Type, the wire electrode and workpiece are fully submerged in dielectric fluid, which helps in better insulation and cooling effects during the cutting process.</p></p>

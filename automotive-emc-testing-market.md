<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Automotive EMC testing market includes various types of tests to ensure that electronic devices in vehicles meet the required electromagnetic compatibility standards. These tests include Bulk Current Injection (BCI) to inject current to simulate high-frequency noise, Conducted Emissions (CE) to measure the electrical noise produced by the device, Conducted Transient Emissions (CTE) to test for transient disturbances, Conducted Transient Immunity (CTI) to simulate transient noise immunity, Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) to test for electrostatic discharges, Radiated Emissions (RE) to measure radio frequency emissions, and Radiated Immunity (RI) to test for immunity against RF interferences.</p></p>

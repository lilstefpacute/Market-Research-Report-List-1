<p><p>The aqueous acrylic binders market consists of four types: solution type, dispersion type, emulsion type, and 100% polymer liquid type. Solution type binders are a mixture of acrylic polymers dissolved in water. Dispersion type binders contain small acrylic particles dispersed in water. Emulsion type binders have larger acrylic particles dispersed in water. Lastly, 100% polymer liquid type binders are pure acrylic polymer liquids without any additional water or solvents. These different types cater to various applications and provide different qualities such as adhesion, durability, and film-forming properties.</p></p>

<p><p>The metallic gasket and seal market consists of various types, such as spiral wound, metal jacketed, and others. Spiral wound gaskets are made by winding metallic strips and sealing fillers together, offering high temperature and pressure resistance. Metal jacketed gaskets consist of a soft filler material encased in a metallic jacket, providing better protection against corrosion. Other types of metallic gaskets and seals include flat gaskets, ring gaskets, and kammprofile gaskets, each suited for different applications based on factors like temperature, pressure, and media type.</p></p>

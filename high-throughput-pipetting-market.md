<p><p>The high-throughput pipetting market includes two main types: manual pipetting systems and electric pipetting systems. Manual pipetting systems require human involvement and are operated by hand, typically used in smaller laboratories or for low-throughput applications. Electric pipetting systems, on the other hand, are automated devices that offer higher precision and efficiency for larger laboratories or high-throughput applications. Both systems are used in the scientific industry for liquid handling, but electric pipetting systems provide advantages in terms of accuracy, reproducibility, and speed.</p></p>

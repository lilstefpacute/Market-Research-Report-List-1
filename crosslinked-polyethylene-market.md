<p><p>Crosslinked Polyethylene (PEX) is a versatile material used for various applications in the construction, automotive, and electrical industries. It is manufactured through different crosslinking methods, including Silane Cross-linked, Peroxide Cross-linked, and Radiation Cross-linked. Silane Cross-linked PEX is created by a process where a material is exposed to moisture and a silane compound. Peroxide Cross-linked PEX is produced by adding a peroxide catalyst during the manufacturing process. Radiation Cross-linked PEX is achieved by exposing the material to high-energy radiation. Each crosslinking method offers unique properties to cater to specific application requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>Non-mydriatic fundus cameras are medical devices used for capturing images of the back portion of the eye known as the fundus, without the need for the dilation of pupils. These cameras use infrared technology to capture high-resolution images of the retina, optic disc, macula, and blood vessels. They are widely used in the diagnosis and screening of various eye diseases such as diabetic retinopathy, glaucoma, macular degeneration, and retinal detachment.</p><p>The non-mydriatic fundus cameras market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.

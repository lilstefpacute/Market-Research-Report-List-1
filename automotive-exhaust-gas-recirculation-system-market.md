<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) System market consists of two types of EGR valves: Pneumatic EGR Valve and Electric EGR Valve. </p><p>Pneumatic EGR Valves regulate the flow of exhaust gases by using pneumatic actuators to open or close the valve. They are commonly used in older vehicles and have been reliable and cost-effective.</p><p>On the other hand, Electric EGR Valves use electronic actuators to control the flow of exhaust gases. They are more precise in regulating the gas recirculation and can be easily integrated into modern vehicle systems, allowing for better fuel efficiency and reduced emissions.</p></p>

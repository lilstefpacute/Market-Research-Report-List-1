<p><p>Screen print labels are labels that are created using the screen printing process. One of the key drivers for this growth is the increasing demand for custom printed labels in various industries such as food and beverage, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and automotive. Customization allows brands to create unique and eye-catching labels that can help to differentiate their products in a crowded marketplace.</p><p>Additionally, screen print labels offer several advantages over other labeling methods. They can be applied to a wide range of materials, including plastics, metals, and glass, making them versatile for different packaging types. Screen printing also allows for precise color matching, ensuring consistency across different label designs.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing preference for sustainable packaging solutions, which includes eco-friendly labels. <p><p>The screen print label market consists of various types. Pressure sensitive labels are adhesive-backed labels that stick to a product's surface when pressure is applied. Glue-applied labels require the use of glue to bond them to the product's surface. Heat-shrink and stretch sleeve labels are designed to fit tightly around a container by using heat to shrink them or by stretching them. In-mold labels are applied during the manufacturing process, becoming an integral part of the container itself. These labels serve different purposes and cater to specific labeling needs in the market.</p></p>

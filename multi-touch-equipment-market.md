<p><p>The multi-touch equipment market includes various types of technology used for touch-sensitive interfaces. Solenoid actuation involves the use of electromagnetic fields to generate touch response. Piezoelectric actuation uses crystals that generate electric charges and vibrations when pressure is applied. Ciliated surfaces use tiny hair-like structures that sense touch. Pin matrices consist of an array of pins that move up and down to create touch feedback. These technologies enable interactive touchscreens and touchpads in devices like smartphones, tablets, and interactive displays.</p></p>

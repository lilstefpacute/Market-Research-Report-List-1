<p><p>The commercial vehicle mirror market consists of two types: internal mirrors and external mirrors. External mirrors, on the other hand, are positioned outside the vehicle and provide the driver with a wider range of vision, including the sides and rear of the vehicle. These mirrors are essential for commercial vehicles as they enhance driver's visibility and contribute to overall road safety.</p></p>

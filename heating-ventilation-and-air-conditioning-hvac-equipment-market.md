<p><p>The HVAC equipment market consists of various types, including Split HVAC Systems, Hybrid Split HVAC Systems, Duct-Free HVAC Systems, and Packaged Heating and Air HVAC Systems. Split HVAC Systems have the compressor and condenser unit located outside the building, while the evaporator unit is situated inside. Hybrid Split HVAC Systems work similarly but can switch between electric and fossil fuel heating. Duct-Free HVAC Systems are ideal for spaces without ductwork. Packaged Heating and Air HVAC Systems combine all components into a single unit, typically installed on roofs or ground level.</p></p>

<p><p>The Agricultural Wastewater Pumps market consists of two main types: Non-submersible pumps and Submersible pumps. </p><p>Non-submersible pumps are designed to be placed outside the water source and transfer wastewater through a pipe. They are commonly used for pumping large volumes of agricultural wastewater over long distances.</p><p>On the other hand, Submersible pumps are designed to be placed directly into the water source. They are specifically built to handle wastewater while being submerged in it. Submersible pumps are commonly used for applications such as emptying flooded areas or moving wastewater from a lower to a higher elevation.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Display Mask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photomask</li><li>Shadow Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The display mask market encompasses two types: photomask and shadow mask. Photomasks are used in photolithography processes to transfer circuit patterns onto semiconductor substrates for manufacturing electronic devices. They are highly precise and consist of translucent plates with the desired circuit pattern. On the other hand, shadow masks are specifically used in cathode ray tube (CRT) displays, with a perforated metal sheet guiding the electron beam to produce colored pixels on the screen. Both types are crucial for the production and functioning of various display technologies.</p></p>

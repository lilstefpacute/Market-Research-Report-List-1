<p><p>Car headrests come in two main types: integral car headrests and adjustable car headrests. Integral car headrests are fixed and cannot be adjusted; they are typically built into the seats of the car. Adjustable car headrests, on the other hand, can be customized to the preferences of the passengers. These headrests can be moved up or down, forward or backward, providing comfort and support for different individuals. Both types of headrests aim to enhance passenger safety and reduce the risk of neck and spinal injuries in the event of a car accident.</p></p>

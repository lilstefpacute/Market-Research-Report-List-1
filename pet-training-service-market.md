<p><p>Pet training service refers to professional training programs that aim to enhance the behavior and obedience of pets, such as dogs and cats. Pet owners are now more willing to invest in professional training to ensure their pets' well-being and social integration. Factors such as increasing pet humanization, rising disposable income, and technological advancements are driving the market's expansion.

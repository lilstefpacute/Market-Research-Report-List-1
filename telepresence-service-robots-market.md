<p><p>The telepresence service robots market can be divided into two main types: mobile telepresence robots and stationary telepresence robots. Mobile telepresence robots are equipped with wheels or other means of mobility, allowing them to move around and interact with their surroundings remotely. They are commonly used in healthcare, education, and corporate settings to enable remote communication and collaboration. On the other hand, stationary telepresence robots are fixed in a specific location and allow users to virtually "visit" that location by controlling the robot's movements and accessing its video and audio capabilities. These robots are typically used in areas such as museums, retail, and hospitality industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Selenium-rich agricultural products are those that have a high content of selenium, an essential trace mineral that is important for various biological processes in humans and animals. Selenium is known to have antioxidant properties and plays a crucial role in supporting the immune system, regulating thyroid function, and reducing the risk of certain chronic diseases.</p><p>The market is also being driven by factors such as the rising demand for organic and natural products, as selenium is more commonly found in organic soils. Furthermore, the growing emphasis on animal nutrition to enhance productivity and promote animal health is also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Geographically, North America and Europe are the major markets for selenium-rich agricultural products, owing to the high consumption of selenium-rich foods in these regions. However, there is also increasing demand in developing regions like Asia Pacific and Latin America, where selenium deficiencies are prevalent.</p><p>The latest trend in the selenium-rich agricultural products market is the use of biofortification techniques to increase the selenium content in crops and animal products. Biofortification involves breeding crop varieties that have higher levels of micronutrients like selenium. This method provides a sustainable solution to combat selenium deficiencies in regions where soil selenium levels are low.</p><p>In conclusion, the selenium-rich agricultural products market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 8.1% during the forecast period.

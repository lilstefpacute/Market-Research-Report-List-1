<p><p>Copper foil is a thin sheet of copper used in various industries, including the wireless charging market. There are two types of copper foils commonly used in this context: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is made by depositing copper onto a titanium drum through an electroplating process. It is mainly used in high-frequency circuit boards. Rolled copper foil, on the other hand, is made by rolling copper ingots, resulting in a thinner and more flexible foil. It is primarily used in lithium-ion batteries. Both types play crucial roles in enabling efficient wireless charging technology.</p></p>

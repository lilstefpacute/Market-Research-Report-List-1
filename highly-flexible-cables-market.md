<p><p>Highly flexible cables have two main types in the market - double and single. Double market cables consist of two insulated conductors, usually twisted together, to provide enhanced flexibility. They are commonly used in applications where bending and movement are frequent. On the other hand, single market cables have a single conductor, making them suitable for simpler applications where flexibility is not a priority. Both types are used in various industries such as electronics, robotics, automotive, and telecommunications to transmit electric signals efficiently while adapting to dynamic environments.</p></p>

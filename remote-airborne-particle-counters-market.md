<p><ul><li>Ionising</li><li>Non-ionising</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Remote airborne particle counters can be classified into two types: ionizing and non-ionizing market. Ionizing particle counters use the method of charging particles and then measuring the current generated by these charged particles. This method is effective for detecting both large and small particles. On the other hand, non-ionizing particle counters use light scattering technology to detect airborne particles. These counters are ideal for measuring smaller particles and provide accurate and reliable data for air quality monitoring.</p></p>

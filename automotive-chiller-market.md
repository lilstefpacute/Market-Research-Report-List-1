<p><p>The automotive chiller market can be classified into three types: coolant pipe, heat exchanger, and expansion valve. Coolant pipe chillers circulate coolant through the chiller system to maintain the desired temperature. Heat exchanger chillers use a heat transfer mechanism to cool the coolant. Expansion valve chillers control the flow of refrigerant to regulate the cooling effect. These types of chillers are crucial components in automotive systems as they help manage the temperature of various components, ensuring proper performance and preventing overheating.</p></p>

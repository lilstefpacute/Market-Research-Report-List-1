<p><p>A single-toggle jaw crusher is a type of jaw crusher in which the swing jaw is suspended on the eccentric shaft, which leads to a much more compact design than that of the double-toggle jaw crusher. The swing jaw, suspended on the eccentric shaft, creates a toggle plate that applies a compressive force on the rock material during operation. This force is achieved by the vertical movement of the swing jaw as the eccentric shaft rotates.</p><p>The single-toggle jaw crusher is known for its higher throughput capacity than the double-toggle design, which makes it ideal for crushing large, hard, abrasive rocks and ores. The single-toggle design also provides a better nip angle, which enhances the jaw's ability to grip the material and reduce material slippage.</p><p>Market analysis indicates that the single-toggle jaw crusher market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.

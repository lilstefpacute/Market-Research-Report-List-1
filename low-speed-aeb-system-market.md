<p><ul><li>Forward Collision Warning</li><li>Dynamic Brake Support</li><li>Crash Imminent Braking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Speed AEB (Automatic Emergency Braking) System market consists of three main types: Forward Collision Warning, Dynamic Brake Support, and Crash Imminent Braking. Forward Collision Warning warns drivers of a potential collision, while Dynamic Brake Support supplements the driver's braking effort to reduce the impact of a collision if the driver fails to respond. Crash Imminent Braking autonomously applies the brakes to prevent or mitigate the severity of an imminent collision. These systems aim to enhance overall vehicle safety by alerting and assisting drivers in avoiding or reducing the impact of low-speed collisions.</p></p>

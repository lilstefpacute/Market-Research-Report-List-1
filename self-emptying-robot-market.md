<p><p>The self-emptying robot market can be divided into two types: rechargeable and plug-in. Rechargeable self-emptying robots operate on battery power and can be recharged when the battery runs low. These robots offer flexibility in terms of placement as they are not limited by proximity to electrical outlets. On the other hand, plug-in self-emptying robots require a constant power supply through a cord connected to an electrical outlet. While they may be more limited in terms of placement options, plug-in robots provide uninterrupted cleaning as they don't rely on battery life.</p></p>

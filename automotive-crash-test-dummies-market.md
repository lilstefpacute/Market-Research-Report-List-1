<p><p>The automotive crash test dummies market can be categorized into three types: frontal impact dummies, side impact dummies, and rear impact dummies. Frontal impact dummies simulate the effects of a head-on collision and are designed to measure forces on the head, chest, and legs. Side impact dummies are used to evaluate the safety of the vehicle in the event of a side collision, measuring forces on the chest, abdomen, and pelvis. Rear impact dummies focus on simulating rear-end collisions and analyzing forces on the head and neck.</p></p>

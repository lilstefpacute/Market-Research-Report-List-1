<p><p>Automated Sample Digestion Systems are instruments used in laboratories for the decomposition of complex samples into simpler components for analysis purposes. Microwave Digestion Systems use microwave radiation to heat and decompose samples quickly and efficiently. Hotblock Digestion Systems, on the other hand, use electric heating elements to heat samples in a block that is immersed in a temperature-controlled fluid. Both systems offer automated features for precise, reproducible, and time-saving sample preparation, making them valuable tools in various scientific fields.</p></p>

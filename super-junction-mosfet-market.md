<p><p>Super-Junction MOSFET is a type of power semiconductor device used in various applications. The market for Super-Junction MOSFET is segmented into Multiple-Epitaxy and Deep-Trench technologies. Multiple-Epitaxy technology involves the growth of multiple epitaxial layers on the silicon substrate, helping to reduce the on-resistance of the device. On the other hand, Deep-Trench technology involves the formation of deep trenches in the silicon substrate, enhancing the breakdown voltage and reducing the specific on-resistance. Both technologies aim to improve the efficiency and performance of Super-Junction MOSFET devices.</p></p>

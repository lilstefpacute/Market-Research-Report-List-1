<p><p>Polypropylene Fibre is a type of synthetic fiber that is widely used in various industries. There are two main types: Polypropylene Staple Fibre and Continuous Fibre. Polypropylene Staple Fibre refers to short fibers that are measured in terms of staple length and are used primarily in non-woven applications such as filters, carpets, and geotextiles. On the other hand, Continuous Fibre refers to long, continuous strands that are used in applications such as ropes, fabrics, and industrial materials. Both types have their unique properties and applications in the market.</p></p>

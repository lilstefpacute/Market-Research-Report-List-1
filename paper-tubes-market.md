<p><p>The paper tubes market can be categorized into two main types: spiral paper tubes and seamless paper tubes. Spiral paper tubes are made by winding multiple layers of paper around a mandrel, creating a sturdy and economical product. On the other hand, seamless paper tubes are manufactured from a single sheet of paper without any visible joints or seams. This type offers superior strength and a more seamless appearance, making it suitable for high-end packaging and display applications. Both types serve various industries such as textiles, construction, food, and cosmetics.</p></p>

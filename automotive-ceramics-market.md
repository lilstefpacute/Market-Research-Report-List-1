<p><p>The automotive ceramics market consists of two main types: structural ceramics and functional ceramics. Structural ceramics are designed to provide strength and durability to automotive components, such as engine parts and brake systems. These ceramics possess high heat resistance and excellent mechanical properties. On the other hand, functional ceramics are used for specific applications in automobiles, such as sensors, capacitors, and filters. These ceramics enable improved performance, enhanced safety features, and increased fuel efficiency in vehicles. Both structural and functional ceramics play crucial roles in the automotive industry.</p></p>

<p><p>The high-performance motorcycle helmets market is divided into two main types: Full Face Helmets and Open Face Helmets. Full face helmets provide maximum protection as they cover the entire head and face, featuring a chin guard and a visor. They are commonly used by professional riders and those seeking optimal safety. On the other hand, Open face helmets only cover the top and back of the head, leaving the face exposed. They are popular among casual riders due to their lightweight, freedom, and better visibility.</p></p>

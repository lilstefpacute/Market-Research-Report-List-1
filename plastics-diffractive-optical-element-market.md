<p><strong>The Plastics Diffractive Optical Element Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beam Shaping (Top-Hat)</li><li>Beam Splitting</li><li>Beam Foci</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastics diffractive optical element market offers three types of applications: beam shaping (Top-Hat), beam splitting, and beam foci. In beam shaping, the optical element alters the intensity distribution of a laser beam into a specific shape, such as a Top-Hat profile. Beam splitting involves dividing a single laser beam into multiple beams, which can be useful in various industrial processes. Lastly, beam foci concentrates the laser beam at a precise point, allowing for precise material processing and measurement applications. These applications cater to different needs and industries requiring specific laser beam manipulation capabilities.</p></p>

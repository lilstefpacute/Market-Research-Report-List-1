<p><strong>The Lithium Battery Copper Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium battery copper foil market comprises two types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electroplating process and is considered the most common type in the market. It offers high smoothness and uniform thickness, enabling better adhesion between the copper foil and the electrode material in lithium batteries. On the other hand, rolled copper foil is manufactured by cold rolling thick copper plates. It is known for its excellent tensile strength, flexibility, and uniformity of thickness, making it suitable for various lithium battery applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The 3D scanners market comprises various types, such as laser scanners and portable CMM (Coordinate Measurement Machine) based scanners. They are commonly used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and healthcare for quality control and reverse engineering purposes. On the other hand, portable CMM based scanners use a hand-held device to collect surface data of objects, providing flexibility and ease of use. This makes them suitable for on-field data collection and inspection applications.</p></p>

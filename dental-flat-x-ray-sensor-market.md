<p><p>The Dental Flat X-ray Sensor Market includes two types, namely CCD Technology and CMOS Technology. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors are used to convert X-ray photons into electronic signals, producing high-quality images. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors are known for their lower power consumption and faster data transfer rates. Both types offer advantages such as improved image resolution, reduced radiation exposure, and enhanced patient comfort. These technological advancements play a significant role in the dental industry by enabling accurate diagnoses and effective treatment planning.</p></p>

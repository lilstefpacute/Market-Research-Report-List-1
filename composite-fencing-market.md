<p><p>Composite fencing is a type of fencing material made from a combination of wood fibers, recycled plastic or vinyl, and binding agents. One of the key drivers for this growth is the increasing demand for eco-friendly and sustainable fencing options. Composite fencing fits this requirement as it is made from recycled materials, reducing the need for new wood and plastic resources. Additionally, composite fencing does not require painting or staining like traditional wood fencing, resulting in lower maintenance costs and efforts.</p><p>The growing construction industry is another factor contributing to the growth of the composite fencing market. As new residential and commercial properties are being built, the demand for fencing materials is also increasing. Composite fencing offers a durable and aesthetically pleasing option for property owners, driving its adoption in the market.</p><p>Furthermore, the durability and resistance to weathering and decay of composite fencing make it a viable choice for outdoor applications.

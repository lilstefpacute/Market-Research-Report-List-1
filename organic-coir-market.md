<p><p>The organic coir market consists of two main types: white fiber and brown fiber. White fiber is obtained from immature coconuts, while brown fiber is derived from mature coconuts. White fiber is known for its softness and high water-holding capacity, making it ideal for use in mattress filling, upholstery, and erosion control products. On the other hand, brown fiber is more durable and resistant to microbial attacks, making it suitable for applications such as mats, ropes, and brushes. Both types of fibers are widely used in the organic coir industry for various purposes.</p></p>

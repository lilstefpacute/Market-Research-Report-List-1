<p><p>Quasi Continuous Wave (QCW) fiber lasers are a type of laser that operate in a pulsed mode, but with a higher repetition rate compared to traditional pulsed lasers. The increasing demand for efficient and cost-effective lasers in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics is driving the market growth. QCW fiber lasers offer advantages such as high peak power, high beam quality, and high repetition rate, which make them suitable for applications such as laser cutting, welding, and drilling.</p><p>Moreover, the adoption of QCW fiber lasers in medical applications, such as ophthalmology and dermatology, is also contributing to the market growth.

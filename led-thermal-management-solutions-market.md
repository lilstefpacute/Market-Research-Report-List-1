<p><p>LED thermal management solutions market types are categorized into several segments based on their functionality and application. </p><p>1. Thermal Clad Board: This type of solution utilizes a PCB with a thermally conductive dielectric layer to efficiently dissipate heat from the LED. The board transfers the heat to a metal core, which aids in heat dissipation.</p><p>2. Thermal Conductive Pad: These pads are soft, highly conductive materials that are placed between the LED module and heat sink. They provide a thermal pathway for heat transfer and help in maintaining optimal temperature.</p><p>3. Liquid Cooling Product: This solution utilizes a liquid cooling system, such as a heat pipe or liquid-filled heat sink, to efficiently remove heat from the LED. The liquid absorbs the heat and transfers it away from the LED.</p><p>4. Other market types: This category includes various alternative solutions such as heat sinks, fans, phase change materials, and heat spreaders, which are used to dissipate heat from LEDs. These solutions help in maintaining the temperature within safe operating limits and prolong the lifespan of LEDs.</p></p>

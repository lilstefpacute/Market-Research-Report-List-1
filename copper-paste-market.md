<p><p>Copper paste is available in three main types: Low Temperature Sintered, Medium Temperature Sintered, and High Temperature Sintered. Low temperature sintered copper paste is suitable for applications where temperature resistance is not critical. Medium temperature sintered copper paste offers improved temperature resistance and is commonly used in electronic devices and electrically conductive adhesives. High temperature sintered copper paste is specifically designed for applications that demand excellent temperature resistance, such as in automotive electronics or power modules. These different types of copper paste cater to varying temperature requirements in diverse industries.</p></p>

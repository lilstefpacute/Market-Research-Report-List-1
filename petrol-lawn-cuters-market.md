<p><p>The petrol lawn cutters market consists of two main types: self-propelled driving and push driving. Self-propelled driving lawn cutters are equipped with a motor that powers the wheels, allowing them to move forward without requiring manual pushing. This type is suitable for larger lawns or for users who prefer a more effortless cutting experience. On the other hand, push driving lawn cutters require manual force to push and move them forward. These are typically used for smaller lawns or by individuals who prefer a more traditional and hands-on approach to lawn care.</p></p>

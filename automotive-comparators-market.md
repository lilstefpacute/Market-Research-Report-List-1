<p><p>Automotive comparators refer to devices used in automobiles to compare electrical signals and voltages. The market types are categorized based on the number of channels each comparator has. A 4-channel comparator has the ability to compare four different signals simultaneously, making it suitable for complex systems with multiple inputs. A 2-channel comparator can handle two signals at once, offering flexibility for moderate applications.

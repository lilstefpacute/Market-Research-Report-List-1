<p><p>Packaging adhesive film is available in several types based on the material used. Polypropylene (PP) adhesive film offers excellent clarity, moisture resistance, and high tensile strength. Polyethylene (PE) adhesive film is known for its flexibility and elasticity, making it suitable for various packaging applications. Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) adhesive film provides superior durability and heat resistance. Other types, including PET (polyethylene terephthalate), PU (polyurethane), and PA (polyamide), offer specific properties like transparency, chemical resistance, and thermal stability. These adhesive films cater to diverse packaging needs in industries such as food, healthcare, and consumer goods.</p></p>

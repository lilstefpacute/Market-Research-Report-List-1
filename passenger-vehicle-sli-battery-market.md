<p><p>The passenger vehicle SLI (Starting, Lighting, and Ignition) battery market consists of various types. Flooded type batteries are traditional batteries that require regular maintenance and periodic refilling with distilled water. Maintenance-free type batteries, on the other hand, do not require any maintenance and are sealed, making them more convenient for users. The "others" category includes newer technologies such as AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and EFB (Enhanced Flooded Battery) that offer improved performance and longer lifespan compared to traditional flooded type batteries.</p></p>

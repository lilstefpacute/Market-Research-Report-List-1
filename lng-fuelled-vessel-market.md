<p><p>A LNG fuelled vessel, also known as a liquefied natural gas fuelled vessel, is a type of ship that utilizes LNG as its primary fuel source. LNG fuelled vessels are becoming increasingly popular in the maritime industry as a more environmentally friendly alternative to traditional fuel options such as diesel or heavy fuel oil.</p><p>The future outlook for the LNG fuelled vessel market is positive, with significant growth expected in the coming years. The increasing focus on reducing greenhouse gas emissions in the shipping industry, along with stricter regulations on maritime pollution, has led to a rising demand for cleaner fuel alternatives. LNG, being a low carbon fuel, offers several benefits such as reduced emissions of sulfur oxides, nitrogen oxides, and particulate matter. These advantages have propelled the adoption of LNG fuelled vessels across various segments, including commercial shipping, cargo transportation, and offshore support vessels.</p><p>Additionally, the global LNG infrastructure is rapidly expanding, providing a steady supply of LNG fuel to support the growth of LNG fuelled vessels. The development of LNG bunkering facilities, which enable the refueling of LNG fuelled vessels, has further boosted the market. Several countries, such as Japan, Norway, and the Netherlands, have made significant investments in LNG bunkering infrastructure to support the use of LNG as a marine fuel.</p><p>Overall, the LNG fuelled vessel market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years.

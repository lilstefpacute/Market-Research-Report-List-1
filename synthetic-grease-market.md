<p><p>Synthetic grease is categorized into four market types based on its composition. Inorganic synthetic grease is made from non-carbon-based materials and offers good resistance to high temperatures. Organic synthetic grease is derived from carbon-based materials and provides excellent lubrication and compatibility with other materials. Hydrocarbon synthetic grease is formulated using hydrocarbon base oils and is widely used in various applications due to its stability and resistance to oxidation. Lastly, the "other" market type refers to the miscellaneous synthetic greases that do not fit into the aforementioned categories, such as silicone or fluorinated greases.</p></p>

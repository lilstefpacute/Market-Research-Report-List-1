<p><strong>The Aircraft Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Dynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft seals market consists of two main types: static seals and dynamic seals. Static seals are used to prevent the leakage of fluids or gases in stationary or non-moving parts of an aircraft, such as doors or access panels. They provide a reliable barrier to maintain the integrity of the enclosed compartments. In contrast, dynamic seals are utilized in moving parts, such as landing gear or engine components. These seals are designed to withstand continuous motion while still preventing fluid or gas leakage, contributing to the overall safety and efficiency of the aircraft.</p></p>

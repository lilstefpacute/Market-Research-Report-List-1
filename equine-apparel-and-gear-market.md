<p><p>Equine apparel and gear refers to the clothing and accessories designed for both horses and riders. Firstly, the increasing popularity of horse riding as a recreational activity and competitive sport has surged the demand for specialized apparel and gear. Riders are increasingly seeking stylish and trendy apparel that not only enhances their performance but also reflects their personal style.

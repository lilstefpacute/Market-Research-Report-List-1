<p><p>The automotive transmission drive chain market consists of various types of chains: roller chain, leaf chain, flat-top chain, and others. Roller chain is a popular type that uses cylindrical rollers connected by inner links, offering high load capacity and durability. Leaf chain is designed with interconnected links that are longer and narrower, providing excellent resistance to wear and fatigue. Flat-top chain offers a flat surface, ensuring smooth and precise movement in conveyor systems. Other types include silent chain, bushed chain, and engineer class chain, which cater to specific automotive transmission applications.</p></p>

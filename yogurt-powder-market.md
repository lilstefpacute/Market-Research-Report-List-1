<p><p>The yogurt powder market can be categorized into three types: skimmed yogurt powder, semi-skimmed yogurt powder, and whole yogurt powder. Skimmed yogurt powder is prepared by removing the fat content from the yogurt, making it low in calories and suitable for individuals on a low-fat diet. Semi-skimmed yogurt powder contains a moderate amount of fat, providing a balance between taste and health benefits. Whole yogurt powder retains all the natural fat content, making it rich and creamy in taste. Consumers can choose the type of yogurt powder based on their dietary preferences and taste preferences.</p></p>

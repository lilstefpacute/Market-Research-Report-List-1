<p><p>Defoamers for papermaking are chemical additives used to eliminate or suppress the formation of bubbles during the papermaking process. There are two types of defoamers: ones that eliminate bubbles and ones that suppress bubbles. Defoamers that eliminate bubbles act quickly to break down the foam and prevent its reformation. On the other hand, defoamers that suppress bubbles are designed to control and reduce the formation of foam throughout the papermaking process. Both types of defoamers play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient paper production.</p></p>

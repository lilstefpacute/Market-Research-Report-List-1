<p><strong>The Electronic Copper Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic copper foil market consists of two main types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced by rolling copper sheets through high-speed rolling mills to achieve the desired thickness. This type of foil is widely used in printed circuit boards (PCBs) for electronic devices. On the other hand, electrolytic copper foil is manufactured through an electrolysis process, resulting in a more refined and uniform structure. It is commonly used in flexible PCBs due to its superior flexibility and conductive properties. Both types play a crucial role in the electronics industry.</p></p>

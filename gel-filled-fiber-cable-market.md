On the other hand, Multi-Mode cables can transmit multiple rays of light simultaneously, making them suitable for shorter distances such as data centers and local area networks. Both cable types are filled with gel to protect the delicate optical fibers from moisture and other external factors, ensuring reliable and efficient communication.</p></p>

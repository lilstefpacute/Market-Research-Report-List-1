Additionally, the growing emphasis on aerial surveillance and intelligence gathering is likely to contribute to the market's growth.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements such as the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) algorithms into EOPs are expected to drive market growth.

<p><p>Bulk wafer transfer systems are used in the semiconductor industry to transport wafers between different fabrication processes. The market types for these systems include bulk automatic horizontal wafer transfer and bulk manual wafer transfer. </p><p>Bulk automatic horizontal wafer transfer systems are fully automated and utilize mechanical arms or robots to move wafers horizontally from one process to another. These systems offer high efficiency and reliability, reducing the risk of damage to the wafers.</p><p>In contrast, bulk manual wafer transfer systems require human intervention to manually load and unload wafers between processes. These systems are less automated and may have limitations in terms of speed and precision.</p></p>

<p><p>Maleic acid is a dicarboxylic acid with the formula C4H4O4. It is a white crystalline solid that is widely used in various industries such as chemicals, food, and pharmaceuticals. Maleic acid is primarily used in the production of unsaturated polyester resins, which are extensively used in the manufacturing of fiberglass-reinforced plastics and coatings. It is also utilized as a precursor for the synthesis of other chemicals like fumaric acid and maleic anhydride.</p><p>The global maleic acid market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue this trend during the forecast period. The market is driven by the growing demand for unsaturated polyester resins in various end-use industries, such as automotive, construction, and electrical & electronics. Maleic acid's excellent chemical resistance and physical properties make it an ideal choice for producing high-quality resins, thereby driving market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the increasing usage of maleic acid in the food and beverage industry as an acidulant and flavor enhancer is further propelling market growth. Maleic acid is used as a preservative, pH control agent, and flavor enhancer in various food products.

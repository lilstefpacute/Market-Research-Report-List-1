<p><strong>The Automotive Permanent Magnet Synchronus Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface PMSM (SPMSM) Type</li><li>Interior PMSM (IPMSM) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (PMSM) market is segmented into two types: Surface PMSM (SPMSM) and Interior PMSM (IPMSM). </p><p>SPMSM motors have their permanent magnets placed on the rotor's surface, resulting in easier heat dissipation and higher power density. They are commonly used in electric power steering systems and small electric vehicle applications.</p><p>On the other hand, IPMSM motors have permanent magnets located inside the rotor, allowing for better magnet utilization and higher torque density. These motors are typically found in electric vehicle powertrain systems and require a more complex design and manufacturing process.</p></p>

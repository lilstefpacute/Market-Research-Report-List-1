<p><p>Extrusion calibration tanks are essential equipment in the plastic industry used to cool and calibrate plastic profiles during the extrusion process. There are two main types of calibration tanks: vacuum and non-vacuum. Vacuum calibration tanks utilize negative pressure to hold and shape the extruded profile, enhancing precision and maintaining consistent dimensions. On the other hand, non-vacuum calibration tanks use conduction and evaporative cooling to set the profile shape. Both types have their advantages and are chosen based on specific requirements and preferences of manufacturers in the plastic extrusion industry.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Ceramic Chip Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dielectric Chip Antennas</li><li>LTCC Chip Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic chip antennas are categorized into two types: dielectric chip antennas and LTCC chip antennas. Dielectric chip antennas are made from a dielectric material, and they utilize the material's properties to generate the desired frequencies. On the other hand, LTCC (Low Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) chip antennas are created through a specialized process that enables the integration of multiple layers of conductive material on a ceramic substrate, resulting in enhanced performance and compact size. Both types are widely used in various wireless applications due to their small form factor and efficient signal transmission capabilities.</p></p>

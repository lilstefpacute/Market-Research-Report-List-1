Concentrates are mandarin oils with a higher concentration of the essential oil content, making them more potent and long-lasting. Blends refer to mandarin oils combined with other complementary essential oils to create unique fragrances or therapeutic properties. These market types cater to different preferences and allow customers to choose the desired strength, aroma, and benefits of Mandarin oil products.</p></p>

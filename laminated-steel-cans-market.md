<p><p>Laminated steel cans are widely used in the packaging industry due to their durability and protective qualities. There are two main types of laminated steel cans: 2-piece cans and 3-piece cans. </p><p>2-piece cans are made from a single sheet of steel that is formed into both the body and the lid of the can. This type of can offers a seamless appearance and is commonly used for beverages and food products.</p><p>On the other hand, 3-piece cans are made from three separate components: the body, lid, and bottom. These cans are more flexible in terms of size and shape, making them suitable for various products such as paints, industrial chemicals, and powdered goods.</p><p>Both types of laminated steel cans provide excellent protection against moisture, light, and oxygen, ensuring the quality and freshness of the packaged contents.</p></p>

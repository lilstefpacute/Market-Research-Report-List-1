<p><p>Machine vision camera market types include PC systems and smart camera systems. PC systems utilize high-end computers with advanced processors and software to process and analyze images. They offer flexibility and high performance for complex vision applications. On the other hand, smart camera systems are compact and self-contained units with embedded processors and software. They provide a cost-effective and easy-to-use solution for simple vision tasks, with the ability to perform image processing and analysis directly on the camera itself. Both types cater to different needs and applications in the machine vision industry.</p></p>

<p><p>Degreasers are cleaning products used to remove grease, oil, and dirt from various surfaces and objects. There are different types of degreasers available in the market. Environmentally compatible degreasers are eco-friendly and do not harm the environment. Ammonia-based degreasers contain ammonia, which provides excellent grease-cutting ability. Liquid degreasers are in liquid form and are commonly used for general cleaning purposes. Foam degreasers are effective for vertical surfaces and cling to the grease for better cleaning. Powder degreasers are in powder form and are mixed with water before use. Petroleum-based degreasers are made from petroleum solvents and are potent for heavy-duty cleaning tasks.</p></p>

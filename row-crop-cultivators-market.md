<p><ul><li>Mounted</li><li>Trailed</li><li>Semi-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Row crop cultivators are agricultural machinery used for weed control and soil loosening in row crops such as maize, soybean, and cotton. Mounted cultivators are directly attached to the tractor's three-point hitch, offering high maneuverability but limited working width. Trailed cultivators are towed behind tractors, providing greater working width and capacity but reduced maneuverability. Semi-mounted cultivators combine the advantages of both types, being partially attached to the tractor, allowing for increased working width and maneuverability compared to trailed cultivators. The choice between these types depends on the specific needs of the farmer's operation and field size.</p></p>

<p><p>Kitchen cleaning chemicals are categorized into two types: alkali cleaning chemicals and strong acid cleaning chemicals. Alkali cleaning chemicals are used to remove greasy and oily residues from kitchen surfaces. They work by breaking down and emulsifying the grease, making it easier to wipe away. On the other hand, strong acid cleaning chemicals are effective in removing tough stains like mineral deposits and rust from kitchen surfaces. They work by dissolving and breaking down the stains, leaving the surfaces clean and shiny. Both types of chemicals are essential for maintaining a clean and hygienic kitchen environment.</p></p>

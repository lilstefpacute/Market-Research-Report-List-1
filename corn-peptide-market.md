<p><p>The corn peptide market can be divided into four types: liquid, dry powder, polypeptide, and oligopeptide. Liquid corn peptides are in a liquid form and are widely used in various food and beverage products. Dry powder corn peptides are obtained by freeze-drying the liquid form and are commonly used in dietary supplements. Polypeptide corn peptides are longer chains of amino acids and are utilized in the pharmaceutical industry for their therapeutic properties. Oligopeptide corn peptides consist of shorter chains of amino acids and are commonly used in cosmetics and skincare products due to their skin rejuvenating properties.</p></p>

There are four types of TMS devices available in the market: sTMS (single-pulse TMS), pTMS (paired-pulse TMS), rTMS (repetitive TMS), and nTMS (navigated TMS). sTMS delivers a single magnetic pulse, pTMS delivers two pulses in rapid succession, rTMS delivers repetitive pulses, and nTMS combines TMS with navigational techniques for precise targeting. These TMS devices are used for various therapeutic and diagnostic purposes, offering potential benefits for patients with neurological and psychiatric conditions.</p></p>

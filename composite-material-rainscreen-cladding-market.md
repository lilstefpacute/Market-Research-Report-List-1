<p><p>The Composite Material Rainscreen Cladding market is categorized into two types: Stationary Type and Adjustable Type. Stationary Type refers to a cladding system that cannot be altered once installed. It offers stability and durability, making it suitable for permanent structures. On the other hand, Adjustable Type is a cladding system that allows for flexibility and modification even after installation. It provides the option to adjust the angle or position of the cladding panels, making it ideal for buildings requiring frequent modifications or renovations.</p></p>

<p><p>The Anti-sniper Detection System market consists of three main types: Infrared, Laser, and Acoustic. Infrared-based systems use heat sensors to identify differences in ambient temperature, allowing detection of sniper scopes and muzzle flashes. Laser-based systems work by emitting laser beams and detecting reflections to identify the presence of a sniper. Acoustic-based systems use audio sensors to detect and locate the soundwaves produced by sniper rifles. All these systems aim to detect snipers and provide early warnings to prevent potential attacks.</p></p>

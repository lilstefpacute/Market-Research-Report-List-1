<p><p>The subcompact ride-on sweepers market consists of three main types of sweepers: regenerative air sweepers, mechanical broom sweepers, and vacuum street sweepers. Regenerative air sweepers use high-velocity air streams to blast dirt and debris off the ground and into a hopper. Mechanical broom sweepers feature rotating brushes that sweep debris into a tray for disposal. Vacuum street sweepers use strong suction power to collect dirt and debris from the surface and store it in a container. Each type offers different cleaning methods to effectively maintain clean streets and surfaces.</p></p>

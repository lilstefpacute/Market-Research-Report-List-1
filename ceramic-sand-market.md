<p><p>Ceramic sand, also known as ceramic proppant, is a type of sand made from high-quality bauxite clays. It is used as a proppant in hydraulic fracturing, a process that extracts oil and gas from underground formations. Ceramic sand is valued for its excellent crush resistance, thermal conductivity, and chemical stability, making it an ideal choice for fracking operations.</p><p>The global ceramic sand market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The market is primarily driven by the rapidly increasing demand for oil and gas and the rising adoption of hydraulic fracturing techniques.

<p><p>Tenderizing equipment for food markets can be categorized into two types: knives roller type and needles injection type. Knives roller type tenderizers use a series of small blades or spikes that penetrate the meat, breaking down tough muscle fibers and making it more tender. On the other hand, needles injection type tenderizers have multiple thin needles that inject marinades or tenderizing solutions into the meat, enhancing its flavor and tenderness. Both types of tenderizers are designed to improve the quality and texture of meat products in the food market.</p></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The electronic power transformer market can be classified into three types based on the number of stages involved in the transformation process. Single-stage transformers have a single step of conversion, where the input voltage is directly transformed to the required output voltage. Two-stage transformers involve two steps of conversion, where the input voltage is first transformed to an intermediate voltage and then further transformed to the desired output voltage. Three-stage transformers have an additional step of voltage transformation, resulting in more precise output voltage levels.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Barrier Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EVOH</li><li>PVDC</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Barrier resin is a type of material used in packaging to protect products from outside factors like oxygen, moisture, and chemicals. The market for barrier resin can be categorized into three types: EVOH, PVDC, and others. EVOH (ethylene vinyl alcohol) offers excellent oxygen barrier properties, making it suitable for food packaging. PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) provides excellent moisture and gas barrier properties, making it commonly used in packaging for pharmaceuticals and chemicals. The "others" category includes barrier resins like nylon, PET, and polypropylene, which are used for various packaging applications based on their specific barrier properties.</p></p>

<p><p>The microplate sealer market consists of three types: manual, semi-automated, and automated microplate sealers. Manual microplate sealers require the user to manually place individual microplates and apply seals. Semi-automated microplate sealers partially automate this process, aiding in plate alignment and seal application. Automated microplate sealers fully automate the sealing process, featuring robotic arms that handle microplates and apply seals. These different types cater to varying user needs and laboratory requirements, providing efficient and convenient solutions for sealing microplates during scientific experiments.</p></p>

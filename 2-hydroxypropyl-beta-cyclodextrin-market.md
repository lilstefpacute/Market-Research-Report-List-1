This modification enhances its solubility and stability, making it highly versatile in different applications.</p><p>In the pharmaceutical industry, (2-Hydroxypropyl)-Beta-Cyclodextrin is commonly used as a solubilizing agent for poorly water-soluble drugs. It forms inclusion complexes with the drug molecules, improving their solubility and bioavailability. Moreover, it can be used to mask unpleasant tastes and odors of certain drugs.</p><p>In the food industry, (2-Hydroxypropyl)-Beta-Cyclodextrin is utilized as an encapsulating agent for flavors, colors, and fragrances.

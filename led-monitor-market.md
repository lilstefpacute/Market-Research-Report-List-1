<p><strong>The LED Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Lit LED Monitor</li><li>Edge Lit LED monitor</li><li>Full Array LED Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>LED monitors are classified into three types: direct lit, edge lit, and full array. Direct lit monitors use LED lights placed behind the entire screen, offering uniform brightness across the display but limited control over individual areas. Edge lit monitors have LEDs placed around the edges of the screen, resulting in a slim design but potentially uneven lighting. Full array monitors use LEDs uniformly distributed behind the entire screen, offering precise control over individual areas for enhanced contrast and image quality. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>Automatic Testing Systems (ATS) are widely used in the semiconductor industry to ensure the quality and performance of integrated circuits. These systems can be categorized into two main types: wafer automatic testing systems and device automatic testing systems. </p><p>Wafer ATS perform tests on semiconductor wafers during the manufacturing process, detecting any defects or abnormalities. This allows manufacturers to identify and rectify issues before moving forward with production. </p><p>Device ATS, on the other hand, test individual integrated circuits once they are assembled onto devices. They assess various parameters such as functionality, reliability, and performance to ensure that the end product meets the required standards.</p></p>

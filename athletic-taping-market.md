<p><p>Athletic taping is a practice widely used in sports to provide support and enhance performance while preventing injuries. Rigid tape, also known as non-stretch tape, offers maximum stability and immobilization to joints and muscles. Support tape, on the other hand, provides less rigidity and more flexibility, allowing the athlete to maintain some range of motion. Elastic tape is designed to provide support while still allowing movement, making it suitable for dynamic sports. Lastly, other types of athletic taping include kinesiology tape, which aims to improve circulation and muscle function through its unique elastic properties.</p></p>

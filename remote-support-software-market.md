<p><p>The remote support software market offers two types of solutions, namely cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based remote support software operates over the internet and is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access it from anywhere with an internet connection. It offers scalability, ease of use, and flexibility. On the other hand, on-premises remote support software is deployed on the organization's local infrastructure. This offers more control over data security and customization options but requires upfront investment in hardware and maintenance.</p></p>

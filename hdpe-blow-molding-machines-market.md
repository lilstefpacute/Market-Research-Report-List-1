<p><p>HDPE blow molding machines are used to shape HDPE (high-density polyethylene) materials into various forms. There are three main types of machines in the market: extrusion blow molding machines, injection blow molding machines, and others. Extrusion blow molding machines heat the HDPE material and use continuous extrusion to produce hollow plastic products. Injection blow molding machines melt the HDPE material and inject it into a mold, resulting in a seamless product. The "others" category includes alternative methods that may involve a combination of these techniques or different processes altogether.</p></p>

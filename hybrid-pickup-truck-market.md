<p><p>Hybrid pickup truck market comprises three main types: Plug-in Hybrid Pickup Trucks, Mild Hybrid Electrical Pickup Trucks, and Full Hybrid Electrical Pickup Trucks. Plug-in hybrid trucks can be charged externally and run mostly on electricity, utilizing a small conventional engine for longer distances. Mild hybrid trucks have a small electric motor and cannot be charged externally, assisting the engine to improve fuel efficiency. Full hybrid trucks have a larger electric motor and can operate at low speeds on electricity alone, seamlessly switching to the internal combustion engine as needed.</p></p>

<p><p>Process chemicals for water treatment are essential in ensuring the quality and safety of water used in various industries. These chemicals can be classified into several types based on their intended purpose. Corrosion inhibitors protect metal components from corrosion, while dispersants prevent the formation of solids in water. Scale inhibitors prevent the accumulation of scale deposits, and fungicides inhibit the growth of fungi and bacteria. Flocculants aid in the coagulation and settling of solids, while cleaners help remove organic and inorganic contaminants. Pretreatment filming agents form a protective film on surfaces, anti-foaming agents reduce foam formation, and decoloring agents remove color impurities. There are also other chemical products available in the market designed for specific water treatment requirements.</p></p>

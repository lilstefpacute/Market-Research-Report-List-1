<p><p>The industrial market for aluminum foil can be classified into three types based on gauges: heavy gauge foil, medium gauge foil, and light gauge foil. Heavy gauge foil refers to thicker foils that are commonly used for insulation, packaging, and industrial applications. Medium gauge foil has moderate thickness and finds applications in areas like automotive, construction, and HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning). Light gauge foil, which is the thinnest among the three, is widely used in household and food packaging industries due to its flexibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>

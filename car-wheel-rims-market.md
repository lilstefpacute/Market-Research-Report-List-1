<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The car wheel rims market is divided into two main types: tubeless wheel rims and tube wheel rims. Tubeless wheel rims are designed without an inner tube, allowing the tire to directly fit onto the rim. This design offers better safety, as it minimizes the risk of sudden deflation. On the other hand, tube wheel rims require an inner tube to be placed inside the tire and then mounted on the rim. While tube wheel rims are less expensive, they are also more prone to sudden punctures or deflation.</p></p>

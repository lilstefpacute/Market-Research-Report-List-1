<p><strong>The Centrifugal Water Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Overhung Impeller</li><li>Vertically Suspended</li><li>Between Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The centrifugal water pump market consists of three main types - Overhung Impeller, Vertically Suspended, and Between Bearing pumps. Overhung Impeller pumps have the impeller mounted at one end of the pump shaft, with the drive motor positioned on the same side. Vertically Suspended pumps have the impeller placed at the bottom, while the motor is positioned above it. Between Bearing pumps have two bearings supporting the pump shaft, with the impeller located in the middle. These types differ in their design and usage, catering to various applications in the water pump market.</p></p>

<p><p>The PH Sensor Cartridge market comprises two types: Single Junction PH Sensor Cartridge and Double Junction PH Sensor Cartridge. Single Junction PH Sensor Cartridges are equipped with a reference electrode and a pH-sensitive electrode in a single chamber, allowing for pH measurements in various solutions. On the other hand, Double Junction PH Sensor Cartridges have an additional chamber with a salt bridge, providing extra protection against sample contamination and electrode poisoning. These different types cater to the diverse needs of industries, laboratories, and environmental monitoring applications in measuring pH levels accurately and reliably.</p></p>

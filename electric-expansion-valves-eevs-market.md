<p><p>Electric Expansion Valves (EEVs) are commonly used in refrigeration and air conditioning systems to regulate the flow of refrigerant. There are two main types of EEVs in the market - Electric Type EEVs and Electromagnetic Type EEVs. </p><p>Electric Type EEVs are controlled by electric signals, allowing for precise control of refrigerant flow based on the system's requirements. This type of EEV offers high accuracy and stability.</p><p>On the other hand, Electromagnetic Type EEVs use electromagnetic forces to regulate refrigerant flow. They are characterized by their quick response time and ability to handle a wide range of refrigerant flow rates.</p><p>Both types of EEVs contribute to improved efficiency and performance of refrigeration and air conditioning systems.</p></p>
